Water Conservation Tips
🚿
Store water during supply hours Fill tanks during scheduled supply (typically 6–8 AM and 5–8 PM).
🔧
Fix household leaks promptly A dripping tap wastes up to 20 litres per day. Report pipe leaks to SMC.
💡
Avoid wastage during low pressure If pressure is low, defer high-flow activities so tail-end households receive water.
